The Electoral Palace in the center of Bonn was the residence of the Cologne Elector. Inextricably linked to the building is the Bonner Hofgarten, a large park located south of the castle.

Old Town Hall

The Old Town Hall on Bonn's market square was built in 1737-38 in the style of the Rococo by the elector court architect Michael Leveilly; However, it was not completely completed until 1780.

Castle Drachenburg

Castle Drachenburg was built in record time from 1882 to 1884 in the style of historicism (especially Neofr체hgotik on the exterior, as well as neo-renaissance in the interior) as a representative residence for Stephan von Sarter.

Castle Drachenburg

In 1986 castle Drachenburg was listed as a historical monument and in 1990 it was placed under the North Rhine-Westphalia Foundation for Nature Conservation, Homeland and Cultural Care. From 1995 to 2010, it was restored based on the original state.

Market Square Old Town

The Bonn market square (official address: Markt) is a place in Bonn. It was built in the 11th century as the center of a commercial settlement on a busy road through Bonn.

Post Tower

The Post Tower in Bonn is the headquarters of the logistics group Deutsche Post DHL Group with its combined business units Deutsche Post and DHL. At 162.5 meters, it is the tallest German skyscraper outside Frankfurt am Main.
